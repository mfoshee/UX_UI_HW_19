Graphic Design & Art Direction

For more than 15 years, I have been a visual communicator. My projects have encompassed the gamut of applications: reports, collateral material, exhibits, identity systems, infographics, presentations, website content, and more.

I have been involved in all aspects of the design process and have worked both independently as well as leading teams. I am process-driven and thrive on a good challenge.

Examples

Branding

Branding is about more than just a logo. A company’s message must be unified in all ways they present themselves publicly.

For The Children’s Guild Alliance, I completed a cohesive rebranding for the organization’s 16 disconnected entities, which included a new style guide and full website redesigns and had been in the works for 9 years when I came on board and connected the team’s goals.

Examples

Marketing

Social media, print ads, website banners, billboards, sales sheets, conference materials, and more need special attention to content to be effective. Too much copy, not enough information, confusing imagery, misrepresentation, or inconsistency can all ruin a great marketing campaign. I am experienced in designing, planning, and implementing effective marketing campaigns.

Examples

Illustration

Whether using traditional methods or digital, illustration has been a passion since I started my career. I am published by such clients as National Geographic, Smithsonian, National Park Service, EPA, and many more. I love the challenge of telling a story without words. Who is my audience? What is the message? What is the brand’s style?

Examples

Fine Art

I love to get messy! Painting and drawing allow me to pull my sleeves up and immerse myself in my process and my subjects. I work in a variety of media, including watercolor, acrylic, oil, pastel, pen & ink, charcoal, and graphite – as long as it makes a mark, I will work with it!

I primarily paint from life or my experiences and generally love to paint animals, people, and landscapes. Travel feeds my soul and I translate those experiences into my art work.
